## **What Is the INF Platform?**

The INF Platform is a centralized digital space designed to streamline every aspect of the Internship & Networking Forum experience.

It serves two primary audiences:

### **For Students**
- Explore internships  
- Register for events  
- Access resources, workshops, and updates  
- Connect with participating companies  
- Build your professional profile  

### **For Companies**
- Showcase company profiles  
- Post internship opportunities  
- Manage applications  
- Connect with SHBM talent  

---

## **Why We Built This Platform**

To move beyond a simple event and create a structured, modern, scalable system that:

- Centralizes all applications  
- Improves student–industry matching  
- Enhances networking engagement  
- Simplifies communication  
- Provides analytics and insights  
- Ensures continuity and long-term impact  

This digital evolution reflects SHBM’s commitment to innovation at UM6P.

---

## **Our Vision**

To transform the INF into a modern, AI-ready, scalable platform that facilitates:

- Professional growth  
- Industry collaboration  
- Talent development  
- Long-term partnerships  

All while preserving the human connection at the heart of hospitality.
